Playable flow now supported
- Start from title and choose `NEW GAME`
- Walk through the starter town area
- Enter grass tiles to roll a wild encounter
- On encounter, transition into `BattleState`
- Choose `WIN` or `RUN`
- Return cleanly to the same world session after battle
- Existing pause and NPC dialogue loops still remain available

Map/layout cleanup
